Game Rules:

Want to test your luck with ADSactly Dice? You can wager Ethereum and roll your way to riches. You can select a range of values (minimum 6) and you can start guessing a number. If you guess the number correctly, you get a payout of (guess * range) = payout. If you wish to buy a multiplier you can increase your potential payout. If you wish to increase your payout, you can buy a multiplier. Every time you buy a multiplier you x2 your potential payout. Multipliers are good for 3 rolls.
